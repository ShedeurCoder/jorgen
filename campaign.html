Jorgen Wurtz VIII Presidency

Former governor Jorgen Wurtz and former Legoland City mayor James Williams are running for president and vice president. Under the Wurtz presidency, we plan to improve Toyland just like how we improved Legoland.

Be a part of the JW club!

Right now, Wurtz · Williams has only about 35% of total support. We would love if you joined the JW club. If we want to make a better Toyland, we must win this election. We cannot, should not, will not let Toyland fall into the hands of an unjust ruler. We shall.

To join the JW club, all you need to do is contact JWClub@JorgenWurtz.com and you will be officially part of the JW Club. If you join the JW Club, you will get a free Wurtz · Williams shirt signed by Wurtz and Williams themselves.

Our slogan

Our slogan "We shall" is a reference to the first Jorgen Wurtz campaign, when Jorgen Wurtz I used this slogan for his campaign. Today is a different day than before, though. We aim for the better, not the worse. We want "We shall" to have a different meaning, symbolising our hard work for a better Toyland. I believe that just because something in the past has a bad connotation, doesn't mean it can't be reclaimed for the better. We shall.
