Politics

My Political View

I consider myself to be a center progressive. I have always wanted the world to be better by advancing as a society as one. I believe that with enough work, every ugly bit of society will be Thanos-snapped out of existance. Even though I believe this, I do believe that this will take decades, maybe even centuries. Therefore, my foundation focuses on continuing my agenda until it is achieved.

How I became anti-Jorgen

My political interest started when I was born. Due to my father and his forefathers being... highly influential political figures in history (in a bad way), I obviously was forced into politics. I had quite the political opinion as a child. Because of my forefather's propaganda, I believed that Toyland was terrible, until we came and made it a piece of glory and greatness, then they fought a war against us and made Toyland terrible again.

Of course, I did not question that narative until much later when I decided to hear what the Toylanders thought about Jorgenberg. I learned about the history of it on Youtube in secret (due to me being the heir, I had been allowed to use Youtube, despite it being banned in former Jorgenberg), and after a secret trip to Toyland to interview citizens there, I came up with the conclusing that Jorgenberg was the terrible one, not Toyland.

My political Career

My political career began when my father was sick and I had to replace him for a while. For 15 days, I was supreme leader. My father survived his sickness, but during those 15 days, I knew how it was to be in power, and was more exposed to the horrors behind Jorgenberg due to me being the leader.

When my father died and I officially became supreme leader of Jorgenberg for life, I immediatley knew I would have to undo every mistake my forefathers did, with the biggest one being Jorgenberg. The first thing I did was provide a more democratic system for the people in 2013. I also did a lot of fundraising for the poverty, and in 2017, I re-united Jorgenberg and Toyland for the first time in over 200 years.

After the two nations re-united, I decided to move to Legoland and enjoy a life in Toyland, a place and culture I was rarely exposed to. After pressure from some of my supporters in Legoland, I decided to run for governor in Legoland. On the second try, I won, and became what professional historian, Jack Maley, the 3rd best governor in all of Legoland history. Sidenote: Jack Maley calls Jorgen Wurtz III the "Worst leader in all of history." I helped lower the poverty percentage by over 40%. I also helped convince the central government to improve the anti-monopolistic acts laws, making small businesses who have no chance with the monopolistic businesses have a bigger change of surviving.
